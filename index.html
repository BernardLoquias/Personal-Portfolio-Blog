About Me

I'm an entrepreneur managing a small podcast where I focus on living your individual day to day lives the right way. I also own a candy brand called "Berni Worms" where I make extremely healthy gummy worms with natural sweeteners and zero artificial ingridients, where my love for gummy worms growing up inspired me to help others enjoy it the right way. I also own an app dedicated in guiding in helping those who are willing to look strong and be strong. This app focuses on making sure people have a good and active lifestyle along with a healthy diet, providing a daily guide for a clear path to success.
